What is Satellite Squad Comics?

Satellite Squad Comics is a small team, in fact, the idea started from two friends! Our goal is to provide engaging content for the love of the genre. Our stories feature our flagship characters Moon-Man and Lunar-Lad amongst others. Similar to the origins of the creators, the characters are from California and many stories will feature familiar landmarks, environments, and cultural references to those who know the area. Inspiration was drawn from some of the biggest superhero teamups present in comics, but see if you can spot any specifics! We hope to provide an enjoyable experience for all readers.

You can expect:

  • Explosive Action!

  • Dynamic Storytelling!

  • Memorable Characters!
